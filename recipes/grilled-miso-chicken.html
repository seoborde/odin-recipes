Grilled Miso Chicken

Description

Easy and extremely flavorful chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Boneless, skinless chicken breast

Ginger Miso Sauce

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp Miso paste
  • 1 tbsp neutral flavored oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili paste

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients for the ginger miso sauce together in a small bowl
  2. Using a meat mallet or tenderizer, pound the chcken breasts to an even thickness
  3. Place the chicken breasts into a large ziploc bag along with the sauce. Rub sauce into the chicken until evenly marinated. Seal and let rest for 2-3 hours in the refrigerator
  4. Heat a grill or pan to high, use generous amounts of cooking spray or oil to not have the miso stick
  5. Place the chicken breasts onto the grill or pan, cooking each side for 4 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
