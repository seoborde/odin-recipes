Okonomiyaki

Description

A quick and easy japanese pancake filled with a variety of ingredients

Ingredients

Okonomiyaki Batter

  • 100g All Purpose Flour
  • 100g Water
  • 1 Egg
  • 3-5 Cabbage Leaves, Chopped
  • Pickled Ginger, Chopped

Toppings

  • 4 Slices of Thinly Sliced Pork Belly
  • Japanese Mayonnaise
  • Okonomiyaki Sauce
  • Bonito Flakes
  • Ao Nori
  • Green Onion, Chopped

Steps

  1. Add the Flour, Water, and Egg to a bowl and mix until smooth
  2. Chop cabbage leaves and pickled ginger into small pieces, then add to the batter
  3. In a large pan, heat oil and add most of the batter to the pan, leaving some in the bowl
  4. Flatten the batter mixture in the pan to a circle, place the pork belly evenly across the pancake, then top with the remaining batter
  5. Cook for 4-5 minutes, until side is golden brown, then flip and cover with a lid
  6. After a few minutes, remove the lid and brush Okonomiyaki sauce onto the surface
  7. Drizzle the japanese Mayonnaise, and top with Bonito Flakes, Ao Nori, and chopped Green Onion
  8. Enjoy!
