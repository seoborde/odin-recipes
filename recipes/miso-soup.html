Miso Soup

Description

Easy nourishing soup with many health benefits

Ingredients

Dashi (makes 4 cups)

  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 piece kombu
  • 1 cup dried bonito flakes

Soup

  • 1 cup Dashi
  • 1 tbsp Miso Paste
  • 1 package of medium to firm tofu, diced into cubes
  • Green Onions, finely chopped

Steps

Dashi Steps

  1. Soak the piece of kombu in 4 cups of water for 30 minutes to overnight
  2. Pour the kombu water mixture into a sauce pain and slowly bring to a boil on medium-low heat
  3. Remove the kombu and discard right before the stock begins to boil
  4. Add the dried bonito flakes and let simmer for 30-60 seconds
  5. Turn off heat and let steep for 10 minutes
  6. Strain over a fine mesh strainer and discard the bonito flakes
  7. Excess Dashi can be stored for up to 3-5 days in the refrigerator

Miso Soup Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of the dashi to a saucepan and bring to a boil
  2. Gently place a ladle with 1 tbsp of miso paste into the stock, mix the paste on the ladle until slowly dissolved
  3. Once miso is completely dissolved, add the diced tofu
  4. Turn off heat before the miso boils
  5. Pour the soup into a bowl and top with finely chopped green onions
  6. Enjoy!
